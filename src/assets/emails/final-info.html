Only a week left until West Coast Spring 2023. In this email you'll find some important information regarding the coming weekend. Please read them carefully, and if you have any questions, don't hesitate to ask on Facebook event or via email.

Check in

Check-in will be possible on the party on Friday night between 21:00 and 24.00 and on Saturday before workshops between 11.00 and 12.00. We only need your name for the check-in. If you haven't sorted out your payment yet, you can also do that in cash upon arrival.

Location

The entire weekend program will happen in the dance school Studio Dansa, located on Vilharjeva 3, 1000 Ljubljana.

You can park on mulitple paid parking lots in the area, the closest one is on Vilharjeva ulica, just across the street. Parking is also possible on the public parking spaces next to the streets if they are marked with white line. On those spaces parking is usually free over weekends, except Saturday mornings. There are parking machines situated on the streets where you can check the free vs. paid hours.

Schedule

The current schedule is available also as an online calendar. You can add it directly through Google calendars or download the ics file, so you can import it into an app of your choice. If the schedule changes, we will update the online calendar first and post them on Facebook event as well.

Private tuition

Daniel and Marina will be available for private tuition during the weekend. The price for the private is 90 eur / hour. It includes the cost of tuition + the cost of the room.

They only have 2 open time slots on Saturday 17:00 - 18:00 and Sunday 15:00 - 16:00, so email us fast to book one. When booking, please include the following information:

  • your full name,
  • name of the instructor,
  • preferred timeslot.

Health and security

We're planning to comply with any health recommendations that may be in place at the time of the event. We kindly ask that you do not attend the event in case you're suffering any respiratory symptoms or have tested positive for the new coronavirus. We will also provide sanitizers, which we encourage you to use regularly.

Please take care of your personal belongings and don't leave them unattended, we take no responsibility for any lost or stolen items.

You participate in the event at your own risk.

Please find here more information about the Event rules.

Please bring your own water bottle and clean dance or indoor shoes.

We're looking forward to welcoming you at our event!
